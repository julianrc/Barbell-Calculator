****INSTRUCTIONS****
Weight is measured in lbs. Empty barbell weight is 45lbs.

Enter 'adder' to measure total weight on loaded bar
      'combinationmaker' to calculate all possible plate combinations for given plate set

adder:
	Enter all plate pairs on 45lb barbell. Ex: For 45lb barbell with 45lb and 25lb plates on each side, enter '45' then '25'

combinationmaker:
	Enter all plate pairs in collection. Ex: For 45lb barbell with 45lb, 25lb, and 35lb available pairs, enter '45' then '25' then '35'.
	All combinations will be printed to console as well as to output.txt file (will overwrite any previous data).
